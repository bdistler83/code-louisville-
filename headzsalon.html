About Us

Headz Salon is full of passionate, creative, and team-oriented people that want to not only help you look your best but also feel your best. Owner Tony Williamson opened Headz in 1985 on the premise of style, fun and family. We strive to make everyone who walks in the door feel like they’re part of our family. We believe in continuing education and staying updated on the latest trends with the highest quality products to keep you out of your style rut! Headz is located in the heart of Lyndon. Come see the care and compassion that makes Headz Salon one of Louisville’s best salons for over 30 years!
